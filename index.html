Help & FAQ
How does Yourdis compare prices?
Yourdis tracks prices across all verified stores in real time and shows you the lowest price available for each fragrance.
How do price alerts work?
Open any product page, set your target price, and we'll notify you the moment it drops to or below that price at any store.
Is Yourdis free to use?
Yes, Yourdis is completely free. We earn a small commission from stores when you purchase through our links, at no extra cost to you.
How does cashback work?
When you shop through Yourdis, you earn 2% cashback on every purchase. It's credited directly to your wallet and can be withdrawn anytime.
How do I add or remove a store from tracking?
Go to the Stores tab and tap Follow on any store you'd like to track. To unfollow, tap the button again on the store's page.
Can I change my language or currency?
Yes. Go to My Profile → Language to switch between Arabic and English, or My Profile → Currency to switch between SAR and USD.
